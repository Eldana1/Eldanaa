Equipments of 3D printer

Extuders : are crucial components in 3D printers . it rejects material in liquid or semi liquid from in order to deposit it in successive layers within in 3D printing volume.
Print bed : is the part that 3D printed object rests on during the printing process.
Hot end : is where the filament is melted then extuded through a nozel.
Filament : is the coil of thermo plastic or a composite that comes in a various diameter.

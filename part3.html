How 3D printing works

3D printing or the process of making a 3D solid object from a digital model is set to revolutionize the way industrise manufacture parts . Here is how it works .

  1. First a3D image is created using a computer aided design .
  2. Then the CAD file is sent to the printer .
  3. The motor draws in to plastic filament melt the plastic and pushes the melted plastic through the nozel
  4. Finly the printer lays down successsive layers of liquid , powder , paper or metal materials and builds the model from a series of crosss sections .
